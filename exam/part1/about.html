The Story Behind Seaside Sizzle
Seaside Sizzle began as a simple dream by a group of beach-loving food enthusiasts who wanted to create a place where anyone could enjoy great seafood without breaking the bank. What started as a modest food stall near the coast grew into a cozy seaside-themed restaurant, where the smell of grilled shrimp and sizzling squid fills the air.

With recipes inspired by coastal towns across the Philippines and beyond, Seaside Sizzle combines local flavors with international favorites. Today, it’s a homegrown brand with a big heart, serving good food with good vibes—just the way the founders envisioned when they first fired up the grill.
